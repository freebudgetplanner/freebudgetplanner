💡 How to Use

  • Plan: First time? Click ➕ Plan New Month to start.
  • Save Session: Use 💾 Save Session to avoid losing work.
  • Save File (Recommended): Click 💾 Save My Budget File to store your plan locally. This file lets you reopen or continue later.
  • Open: Use 📂 to load your saved file anytime.
  • View Plans: After loading, browse saved months instantly.
  • PDF: Click 📄 Get My Budget Copy to export a PDF.
  • Dashboard: Go to 📊 Dashboard to see charts and trends. Use filters to customize the view.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sign up?

No. You can use the planner instantly without any login or signup.

I need help with navigating this website

Absolutely! Please check the Help button on your right.

Is my data stored?

No. Your data is never stored on any server. Everything happens on your device to protect your privacy..

How can I open my Saved Budget?

Yes. You can use the 📂 Open My Saved Budget button to load your saved JSON file and continue budgeting.

Can I download or print my budget?

Yes! You can save a local file and generate a printable PDF version of your monthly budget.

Is it really free?

Yes. Free Budget Planner is 100% free with no hidden fees or premium versions.
